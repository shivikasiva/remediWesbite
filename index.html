About Our App

reMEDI is an app that is focused on making healthcare affordable, personalized and accessible.

Accessible

reMEDI is an application accessible to anyone and anywhere. reMEDI is available for all regardless of nationality, economic status, geographic location or access to health insurance.

Personalized

reMEDI allows individual to recieve specific and detailed feedback relating to a specific personal issue. The app is customized for each user to increase and improve the user experience. One does not have to search for solutions among millions of online platforms and answers and instead is provided with just the one accurate answer they are looking for.

Affordable

reMEDI is available at one's disposal. Individuals no longer have to worry about their hospital bills, transportation to get to medical facilities and acess to healthcare institutions.

Fast

reMEDI ensures the medical help and advise is immediate. Individuals no longer have to schedule an appointment and wait weeks before getting to meet the doctor and address their concerns.

Easy

reMEDI is fully responsive and simple to use making it easy for individuals to easily get help. The app does not require users to have any prior experience with technology. Individuals no longer have to go through tedious documents to be eligible to get access to healthcare.

Free

reMEDI is absolutely free to download. Anyone regardless of economic status can use the app.

How The App Works?

User or Professional

Individuals can choose to use reMEDI either as users seeking medical advise or medical professionals with certified authority who can provide medical help.

Sign Up or Log In

Users and medical professional can choose to sign up if they are new to reMEDI or log in if they already have set up an account earlier using the sign up option.

New Post or View Post

Users can choose new post option to seek medical help by addressing their problem. Medical professionals can view posts by users and provide required assistance.

Get Help or Provide Help

Users can view their previous posts and view the response and medical assistance provided by medical professionals using their reply and comment to user post option. In addition both user and medical proffesionals have an option to communicate with each other in case further assistance is required using messaging option.
